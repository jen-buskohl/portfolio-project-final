Hi! I am Jen Buskohl

I dedicated the last decade of my professional life to the healthcare industry, an area that presented me with diverse challenges and opportunities for personal and career growth. For four years I worked in medical transcription quality assurance, analyzing medical reports for accuracy. Following that, I spent two years in patient registration, where I interacted with patients undergoing cancer treatment, ensuring a seamless and compassionate entry into that healthcare system. The next four years, I delved into the world of human resources, allowing me to hone my customer service skills while navigating the complexities of employee relations and organizational development.

When my position was unexpectedly eliminated, it proved to be a turning point in my career. Instead of feeling discouraged, I chose to see it as an opportunity to explore new paths. I had always been intrigued by web development and decided to switch gears, transitioning towards front-end development. I can't wait to see what this path holds!
